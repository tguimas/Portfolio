Honda Elevate

The Honda Elevate is a subcompact crossover SUV (B-segment) produced by Honda since 2023. It was introduced in India on June 6, 2023, and is also marketed in Japan as the Honda WR-V.

The Elevate is built on the Global Small Car platform which also underpins the seventh-generation City. It is powered by a 1.5-liter petrol engine that produces 89 horsepower and 145 pound-feet of torque. The engine is mated to either a 6-speed manual or a CVT (continuously variable transmission).

The Elevate is available in four main variants: SV, V, VX, and ZX. The SV is the base model, while the ZX is the top-of-the-line model.

Honda SENSING: This suite of ADAS includes lane departure warning, lane keep assist, adaptive cruise control, auto high beams, and forward collision mitigation.

Engine: The Elevate's 1.5-liter petrol engine delivers 89 horsepower and 145 pound-feet of torque, providing ample power for everyday driving needs. It is mated to either a 6-speed manual or a CVT (continuously variable transmission), which offers smooth and efficient power delivery.

Performance: TThe Elevate's performance is responsive and engaging, with a sporty feel that makes it enjoyable to drive. It is also relatively fuel-efficient, with a fuel economy of 15.31 to 16.92 km/l (manual) or 16.92 km/l (CVT).

Technology: The Elevate is also equipped with a range of advanced technology features, including a 7-inch touchscreen infotainment system with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto compatibility, Honda SENSING, and a suite of driver-assistance systems that help to keep the vehicle and its occupants safe.
